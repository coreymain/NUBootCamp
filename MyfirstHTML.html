A Tofino-Worthy Sandwich

  1. Go to the grocery store.
  2. Purchase smoked salmon, goat cheese, chives, arugula, tomato, lemon, salt, pepper, olive oil, and everything Montreal-style bagels
  3. Slice the bagels, tomato, salmon, and the lemon in half.
  4. Chop the chives.
  5. Mix the chives into the goat cheese.
  6. Smear the bottom of the bagel with the goat cheese/chive mixture.
  7. Top with arugula, salmon, tomato
  8. Squeeze the lemon over the top
  9. Drizzle over olive oil and top with salt and pepper.
  10. Cover with the top of the bagel and dig in!
